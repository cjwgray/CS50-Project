Cyberpunk 2077: Phantom Liberty review

Cyberpunk 2077 had a challenging start, yet it resonated with me right from the beginning. Despite numerous bugs and suboptimal optimization, the game offered an unforgettable experience. Its storytelling ranks among the best I've encountered, complemented by exceptional music, compelling characters, and an immersive setting that resonated deeply with me. Personally, Cyberpunk alongside Phantom Liberty stands as one of the finest games of the past decade. I applaud CD Projekt Red for their fantastic work; Cyberpunk is destined to be remembered and replayed for years to come, much like Elder Scrolls and other timeless classics.
